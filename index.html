Welcome to ALPhA Davidson's website

We are the Algorithms for Learning in Physics Applications research group at Davidson College.

ALPhA Summer Positions

  • Summer Research Fellow: Machine Learning for Jefferson Lab
    • Funding source: Department of Energy
    • Project: Neural Networks to predict theoretical structure functions.
    • Description: Use deep neural networks with hidden predictions to predict functions that describe the internal structure of a nucleon (structure functions) from kinematic variables
  • Summer Research Fellow: Machine Learning for the Facility for Rare Isotope Beams
  • Summer Research Fellow*: Machine Learning for Jefferson Lab
    • Funding source: Center for Nuclear Femtography
    • Project: Neural Networks for inverse mapping
    • Description: Use autoencoders and mixture density networks to map experimental nuclear physics data (cross sections) to theoretical parameters in Quantum Chromodynamics

* These funds are not yet confirmed.
